---
title: 'Why Atlas?'
description: 'Atlas discovers structure in your data and give you the tools to use it.'
---

Atlas allows any individual or business to understand large datasets with AI.

Text, images, and lots of other kinds of unstructured data are mappable with Nomic's [models](/guides/quickstart_guide.mdx) that are built into Atlas. 

Atlas finds structure to help you analyze and understand important trends and recurring patterns in your data.

Analysts & data scientists use Atlas to control large datasets for their organizations.

Additionally, Nomic supports AI development and sets the industry standard in developing algorithms for dimensionality reduction and multimodal embedding retrieval.
